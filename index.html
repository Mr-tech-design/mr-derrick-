About Me

The Story Behind the Lens

Derick Shots

Visual Storyteller · Creative Director

I am a passionate visual storyteller dedicated to capturing life's most meaningful moments through the lens. With over 5 years of experience in photography and videography, I bring a cinematic, high-quality aesthetic to every project. My work blends technical precision with creative vision to deliver content that resonates and endures.

From intimate portraits to sweeping cinematic productions, I approach each session with intentionality, care, and a commitment to exceeding expectations. I believe great visuals have the power to inspire, preserve memories, and tell stories that words alone cannot capture.

Mission Statement

"To honor every story I'm trusted to tell — with creativity, integrity, and a heart for excellence."
